Juno is a NASA space probe orbiting the planet Jupiter after entering orbit on July 5, 2016, 03:53 UTC;[4] the prelude to 20 months[6] of scientific data collection to be followed by a planned deorbit.[6] It was launched from Cape Canaveral Air Force Station on August 5, 2011, as part of the New Frontiers program,[7] and ranged into Jupiter's orbit on July 4, 2016.
